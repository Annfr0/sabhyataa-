HEAR WHAT PEOPLE THINK ABOUT US

“This mental health session helped me understand the importance of emotions and emotional behavior in daily life. The best part was receiving a lot of information in a well-structured manner.”

— Rajeev, Student, Jamia Millia Islamia University

Sabhayatta is more than just an organization—it’s a family, a movement, and a source of hope for many. My journey with Sabhayatta began in 2019 when I came across a post on Facebook. Since then, I have been deeply inspired by the dedication and selflessness of its members, especially Geetika, Neha, and Divya. Neha’s unwavering support and Divya’s dedication to educating underprivileged children truly embody the spirit of Sabhayatta. In a world where people often cite busy schedules, work pressure, and financial constraints as reasons to step back, these individuals choose to step forward. Their efforts are not just inspiring—they are transformational.

-A admirer on Facebook

Sabhayatta is building a community of changemakers, just as one well-wisher poetically described: “मैं अकेला ही चला था जानिब-ए-मंज़िल, लोग मिलते गए और कारवां बनता गया।”

Their work aligns with national initiatives for holistic development, including support for transgender individuals and marginalized communities. Their collaborations with stakeholders and grassroots efforts make a real, lasting impact. For me, Sabhayatta is not just an initiative; it’s a revolution of kindness, awareness, and action.

-Volunteer of Sabyataa Foundation
